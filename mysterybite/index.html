FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What do I get inside?+
Every box contains surprise snacks curated by our team of food nerds. You'll receive 5–12 items depending on your plan — everything from artisanal chocolates to spicy chips from countries you've never snacked before.
Can I skip or cancel?+
Yes! Skip a month or cancel anytime from your dashboard. Nostrings, no fees.
Where are snacks sourced?+
We source directly from manufacturers and specialty stores across 40+ countries. Every month's origin changes — that's part of the fun.
What about allergies?+
Set your allergen profile during signup and we'll filter out anything you can't safely eat. Common allergens are flagged on every item card.
Do you ship internationally?+
Currently we ship within the US and Canada. International shipping is on our roadmap for Q4 2026 — join the waitlist to get early access.
